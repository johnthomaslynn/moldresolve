Our Thorough Mold Remediation Process

We leave nothing to chance when it comes to eliminating mold. Every job follows a proven, step-by-step process to ensure the mold is removed safely and your home is restored to a healthy state. Here’s how we work:

Inspection & Assessment: Our certified technicians begin with a detailed inspection of your property to pinpoint all mold-affected areas – even hidden damp spots you might not see. We identify the extent of the infestation and find the source of moisture that’s causing the mold. This thorough assessment allows us to plan the most effective remediation tailored to your situation.

Containment: Once mold is identified, we act quickly to isolate those areas. Using professional containment methods (such as plastic barriers, sealed negative-air chambers, and HEPA air filtration), we prevent airborne spores from spreading to clean parts of your home. By quarantining the affected zone, we keep your family and property protected while we work.

Restoration & Prevention: After the mold is eradicated, we focus on getting your space back to normal and preventing future issues. We thoroughly dry and sanitize the area, and handle any needed structural repairs or replacements of damaged materials. Crucially, we also address the underlying moisture source – whether it’s fixing a leaky pipe, improving ventilation, or dehumidifyg the space – to help stop mold from coming back. The job isn’t done until your home is fully dry, clean, and safe again.
